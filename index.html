My Journey

Arijit Singh (born 25 April 1987) is an Indian playback singer.He sings predominantly in Hindi and Bengali but has also performed in various other Indian languages.Singh is regarded as one of the most versatile and successful singers in the history of Indian music and Hindi cinema.

"If there is one voice that does justice to both love and heartbreak, it is that of Arijit Singh".

I do live shows with other professions in different parts of the world as well as inside the country.The singing Community is awesome and I love this Journey.
